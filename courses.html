Our Courses

  • Introduction to TESOL
    Learn the foundations of Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages, including key methodologies and classroom strategies.
  • Advanced Teaching Techniques
    Explore modern teaching methods, technology integration, and activity-based learning for higher-level classrooms.
  • Classroom Management
    Develop effective approaches for managing classrooms, motivating students, and creating a positive learning environment.
  • Language Assessment
    Master techniques for evaluating student progress, designing assessments, and providing constructive feedback.
  • Professional Development Workshops
    Ongoing training sessions to keep teachers up-to-date with international best practices.
  • Practical Teaching Practicum
    Hands-on experience with real students under expert supervision.
